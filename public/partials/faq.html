Frequently Asked Questions

What is Helper as a Service?

Helper as a Service, or HaaS, is a cloud-based service that provides a personal virtual assistant across multiple devices. HaaS is an open-source community-driven project that aims to simplify everyday tasks.

What can Helper as a Service do for me?

HaaS can do everything current personal assistants can't. Tasks that take significant amounts of time, such as finding the cheapest airfare, can be answered as easily as asking HaaS, "Find me a flight from LAX to SFO in 3 days".
